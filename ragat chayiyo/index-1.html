About Ragat Chaiyo

Ragat chaiyo is a social organization established in 2023 with the aim of creating relation between Blood bank, Blood donor and needy people. In Nepal generally people with less contact or small chain is hard to get instantly blood for their patient,From our web its easy to make contact with blood donor or to see the status of blood in blood bank which helps patients to get blood easily in time and save their life.

Why to donate blood?



  • Saving lives: Donating blood can save the lives of people in need, including accident and surgery patients, cancer patients, and people with blood disorders.
  • Giving back to the community: Blood donation is a way to give back to the community and make a positive impact on the lives of others.
  • Improving health: Regular blood donation can improve the overall health of the donor by reducing the risk of heart disease and cancer.
  • Maintaining a stable blood supply: Blood donation helps maintain a stable blood supply for hospitals and other medical facilities, ensuring that blood is available when needed.
  • You can see the difference you made by knowing that your donation will be used to help someone in need within days.
